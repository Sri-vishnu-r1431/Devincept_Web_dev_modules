Here's a Timeline of Gandhi:

  • 2 October 1869 - Porbandar, India
  • 1883 - "Mahatma Gandhi married to Kasturbai Makhanji Kapadia in May 1883."
  • 4th September 1888 - he left for London for higher studies
  • 1893-1914 - He was a civil right activist in South Africa
  • 1915 -Joined the Indian congress
  • 1917 -Gandhi ji started the first movement against British rule, Champaran Satyagraha
  • 1920 - He launched non-cooperation movement
  • 1930 - He had started Salt/Dandi March on 12th March 1930 and continued till 06th April 1930.
  • 30th January 1930 - Nathuram Godse, a right-wing advocate of Hindu Nationalism, assassinated Gandhi .
